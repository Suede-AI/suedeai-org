The argument in one page.

AI compresses the cost of production, but it does not automatically solve ownership. When everyone can generate more media, the premium shifts to proof: who made it, where it came from, what rights attach to it, whether likeness or voice was authorized, and how value moves when the work travels.

That is the real surface area for Suede. Proof of creation, content provenance, creator ownership, and programmable IP are not decoration around AI media. They are the rails that let creators, rights holders, operators, and agents transact with confidence instead of relying on screenshots, claims, and vibes.
