Creator Ownership

Creator ownership is the category that matters after creation becomes abundant.

Creator ownership becomes more important as AI makes production easier. Scarcity shifts from making more media to proving authorship, controlling rights, and capturing value.

Why it matters

Creation without durable ownership leaves value floating. The more music, images, video, voice, and likeness assets get generated, the more creators need verifiable authorship and programmable control.

How Suede approaches creator ownership

Suede combines proof of creation, content provenance, and programmable rights infrastructure into one ownership layer for creators, platforms, and AI-native media markets.
