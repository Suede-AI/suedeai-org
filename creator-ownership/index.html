Creator Ownership

Ownership is the category that matters after creation becomes abundant.

Creator ownership becomes more important as AI makes creation abundant. Scarcity shifts from production to provenance, identity, and rights.

Why it matters

Creation without durable ownership leaves value floating. The more media gets generated, the more creators need verifiable authorship and programmable control.

How Suede approaches creator ownership

Suede combines proof of creation, provenance, and rights infrastructure into a single ownership layer that can support creators and platforms alike.
