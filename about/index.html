About

Suede Labs AI.

Creator ownership infrastructure for the AI era: proof of creation, programmable IP, music rights, voice and likeness protection, provenance, royalty routing, and agent commerce.

What we build.

Suede Labs AI is the ownership layer for the AI media era. As creation becomes abundant, proof, permission, provenance, and usable rights become the scarce resource. Suede registers creative works, proves authorship, attaches machine-readable rights metadata, routes royalties, and makes intellectual property callable by software agents.

Company.

Suede Labs AI (also known as Suede Labs, Suede AI, or Suede) is a subsidiary of JC Investment Group LLC, founded in 2024. The product layer lives at suedeai.ai and app.suedeai.ai; this site, suedeai.org, carries the company story, founder profile, and public documentation.

Founder

Jason Colapietro (Johnny Suede) is the founder and CEO of Suede Labs AI. He built the creator-ownership layer for the AI media era: proof of creation, programmable IP, on-chain royalty routing, and agent-accessible licensing. Patent pending USPTO 63/947,120. Published author of The Signal Chain, Stake Your Claim, Proof as Infrastructure, The Guitar Without a Number, and The Human Authenticity Layer.
