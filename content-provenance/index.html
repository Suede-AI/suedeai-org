Content Provenance

Content provenance makes authenticity durable at AI scale.

Content provenance records the source, authorship, and ownership context behind media. In the AI era, that proof needs infrastructure, not just platform labels.

Why it matters

As content becomes easier to generate, trust shifts from appearance to lineage. Provenance becomes the bridge between creation, attribution, authenticity, and ownership.

How Suede approaches provenance

Suede treats provenance as part of the ownership stack: proof of creation, rights, identity, and programmable control reinforce one another instead of living in separate systems.
