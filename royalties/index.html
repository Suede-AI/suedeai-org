Automated Royalties

Royalties do not become automatic until ownership becomes verifiable.

You cannot automate royalties on content you cannot verify. Suede starts with proof of creation, turns media into programmable IP, and lets usage trigger attribution, licensing validation, and payment logic.

Why traditional royalty systems break

Traditional royalties rely on manual reporting, delayed platform accounting, opaque intermediaries, and after-the-fact reconciliation. AI increases output, derivatives, and distribution faster than those systems can track.

Proof comes before payment.

Before royalties can work, the system needs a provable creator, a verified creation event, defined usage rights, and royalty logic attached to the asset. Without that foundation, royalties become guesswork.
