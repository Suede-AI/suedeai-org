01 — Thesis

The next internet will be agentic, creative, and owned.

AI systems will create, remix, and transact at scale. Without new infrastructure, value will be extracted, rights will be opaque, and creators will be disconnected from the upside.

Suede Labs is building the ownership layer that makes a better internet possible: verifiable authorship, machine-readable licenses, rights-aware payments, and distribution paths that route value back to the people who create.

A player's history of amplifiers, effects, and the pursuit of electric guitar tone. 46 chapters covering every significant amp and effect in electric guitar history — from the physics of the pickup to the future of modeling.
