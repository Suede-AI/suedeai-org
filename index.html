01 — Thesis

The next internet will be agentic, creative, and owned.

AI systems will create, remix, and transact at scale. Without new infrastructure, value will be extracted, rights will be opaque, and creators will be disconnected from the upside.

Suede Labs is building the ownership layer that makes a better internet possible: verifiable authorship, machine-readable licenses, rights-aware payments, and distribution paths that route value back to the people who create.

02 — Likeness

The whole likeness, on the record.

A generative model can reproduce a face from a press kit and a voice from one verse. That makes identity a commercial surface: every unauthorized render is a licensing event with no record, no consent, and no payment route attached.

Suede treats the whole likeness as catalog. The reference works an identity travels through enter the registry with dated proof and machine-readable consent terms, so an authorized use pays and an unauthorized one meets a public record instead of a creator's word.

  • Voice: a cappellas, demo vocals, isolated stems
  • Face: press photos, album art, portrait sets
  • Name and persona: stage names, aliases, the performed character
  • The work itself: tracks, stems, artwork, video

How likeness protection works  ·  How voice protection works

Identity registry Example session 
> suede register acapella-take3.wav --facet voice --consent no-training
OK sealed sd_2b4f · consent terms attached

> suede register presskit/ --facet face --license commercial
OK 12 reference works sealed

> suede verify suspect-clip.mp4
NO CONSENT ON RECORD · dated evidence: sd_2b4f + 12 works

09 — FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is Suede Labs AI?
Suede Labs is the ownership layer for the AI media era: proof of creation, programmable IP licensing, royalty routing, and agent-commerce-ready endpoints for creators' music and likeness assets.
What does "proof of creation" actually mean?
It's a verifiable record of authorship — who made a work and when — that travels with the work so licensing terms and rights-aware payments can be enforced downstream instead of relying on takedown requests after the fact.
Can Suede protect a voice or likeness from AI cloning?
No registry can stop a model from generating a synthetic voice or face, and Suede does not claim otherwise. Registration changes the evidence position: reference works such as recordings, press photos, and album art carry dated proof and machine-readable consent terms, so an unauthorized clone meets a public record instead of a creator's word. See AI Voice Protection and AI Likeness Protection.
How does agentic commerce work on Suede?
Agents can discover work with verifiable provenance, negotiate licenses programmatically, settle payment, and receive delivery — the full loop from discovery to paid, licensed use. See the agentic commerce docs.
Who is behind Suede Labs?
Suede Labs AI was founded by Jason Colapietro, also known as Johnny Suede, and is a subsidiary of JC Investment Group LLC. Read more on the founder page.
How can I work with Suede?
Investors can request the investor brief, partners can reach out at info@suedeai.ai, and agents/developers can hit the x402 catalog or Agent Studio at agents.suedeai.ai — no key needed to start.
