Suede Studio Voice & Suede Sing Privacy Policy

Suede Studio Voice on Android and Suede Sing on iOS share one architectural commitment: your singing audio never leaves your device. This page explains what that means in practice, what information the apps process, and your rights as a user. Both apps are published by JC Investment Group LLC, a Florida limited liability company doing business as Suede AI ("we", "us", "our").

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. The on-device principle

Suede Studio Voice and Suede Sing perform their singing-voice analysis — including pitch detection, spectral analysis, register classification, range tracking, and recommendation matching — locally on your phone using on-device audio frameworks and classifiers. Microphone audio is never uploaded to any Suede AI server, third-party server, analytics service, advertising service, or cloud service.

The Android app does not request the internet permission and disables Android app backup. The iOS app's App Privacy declaration reflects the same developer-side architecture: "Data Not Collected."

2. What is captured during a session

Microphone audio. During an active range test or practice session, the app records audio from your phone's microphone and processes it locally. Both apps keep local WAV replays by default so you can review individual notes; you can turn off Keep audio recordings or delete all saved recordings from Settings at any time.

Derived measurements. Numeric outputs of the on-device analysis — including range bounds, pitch and stability measurements, register classifications, calibration data, and exercise-completion timestamps — are stored locally in the app's private database so progress and recommendations remain available.

Where they live. The database and replay files live inside the app's private sandbox. Android app backup is disabled for Suede Studio Voice, so its database and replay files are not included in Android cloud backup. Suede Sing explicitly excludes replay files from iCloud and iTunes backup; its local numeric database may be included in your encrypted iCloud device backup when iCloud Backup is enabled. Suede AI cannot access that backup.

3. What the apps do not collect

  • No account is created. There is no signup. There is no login.
  • No name, email address, phone number, or contact information is requested or stored.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, or tracking pixels are integrated into the app.
  • No social-graph access, no contacts access, no photo-library access, no calendar access.
  • No audio is transmitted to any server, including ours.
  • No biometric identifiers are derived or stored.

4. Permissions the app requests

Microphone access. Required to perform singing-voice analysis. Android or iOS displays a system prompt before microphone access is granted. You may revoke it at any time in your phone's privacy settings.

Notifications (optional). If you enable practice reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. Reminder content is generated locally; it is not sent through a Suede AI push-notification server.

5. Network activity

Suede Studio Voice on Android has no internet permission and cannot make network requests. Suede Sing on iOS does not include a network client or a feature that uploads audio, measurements, or other app data. The apps can open external links you deliberately tap — such as this privacy policy, the Terms of Use, or a Suede website — in your phone's browser.

  • The linked website receives ordinary web-request information such as IP address, timestamp, and the page requested.
  • No singing audio, replay file, calibration data, or session measurement is included in that browser request.

6. Third-party services

Neither app integrates advertising, attribution, analytics, crash-reporting, or social-tracking SDKs that collect personal data. Exercise content and audio reference tones are bundled or synthesized on-device.

7. Children

The apps are intended for general audiences, including young singers. Because the apps do not send personal information to Suede AI, there is no Suede-AI-held information about a child to retain, share, or delete. Parents and guardians remain in control of microphone and notification permissions through device settings.

8. Your rights and your data

Because the apps do not collect personal information about you, there is generally no Suede-AI-held data to access, correct, port, or delete on your behalf. On Android, Settings lets you delete saved WAV replays and reset calibration data; numeric session history remains local until you clear the app's storage or uninstall it. On iOS, uninstalling the app removes its local database from the device.

If you have questions about your rights under the CCPA/CPRA, GDPR, or another applicable privacy law as it relates to these apps, email privacy@suedeai.org.

9. Changes to this policy

If a future version introduces a feature that processes data differently — for example, opt-in cloud sync or a coach-share export — we will update this policy and surface the change inside the app before that feature is enabled.

10. Contact

Privacy questions about Suede Studio Voice or Suede Sing? Email privacy@suedeai.org or write to JC Investment Group LLC at the address listed on our contact page.
