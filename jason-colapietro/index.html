Founder Profile

Jason Colapietro is building creator ownership infrastructure for the AI era.

Jason Colapietro is the founder and CEO of Suede Labs, the company behind Suede AI. His work centers on proof of creation, music rights, programmable IP, provenance, licensing, and the ownership layer creators need as media becomes easier to generate and harder to verify.

Jason Colapietro

Founder, Suede Labs. Builder of Suede AI.

Suede is built from a simple thesis: creation is becoming abundant, but ownership still needs proof, permission, provenance, and usable rights.

Jason Colapietro's work connects creator ownership, music rights, voice and likeness rights, programmable IP, licensing, and agent-readable commerce into one infrastructure story. The goal is practical: help creators prove what they made, control how it can be used, and turn rights into something software can understand.
