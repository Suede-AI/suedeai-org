AI Likeness Protection

When identity can be generated, likeness becomes infrastructure.

Likeness protection is a core ownership problem in synthetic media. Without verifiable rights and provenance, identity becomes cheap to copy and hard to defend.

Why it matters

Generative media turns identity into a programmable surface. Protection needs more than moderation workflows; it needs durable ownership rails.

How Suede approaches likeness protection

Suede ties identity claims to proof, provenance, and programmable control, helping likeness function as protected rights-bearing media.
