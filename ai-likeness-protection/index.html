AI Likeness Protection

AI likeness protection starts with ownership, consent, and proof.

When identity can be generated, likeness becomes a rights-bearing asset. Without verifiable provenance and consent, identity becomes easy to copy and hard to defend.

By Jason Colapietro (Johnny Suede) · Founder & CEO of Suede Labs AI

Why it matters

Generative media turns faces, bodies, style, and persona into programmable surfaces. Protection needs more than takedown workflows; it needs durable ownership rails.

How Suede approaches likeness protection

Suede ties identity claims to proof, provenance, consent, and programmable control so likeness can function as protected rights-bearing media.
