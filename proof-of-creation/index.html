Proof of Creation

Proof of creation is the ownership layer beneath AI media.

Proof of creation makes authorship, provenance, and creator rights verifiable when AI makes media abundant. The easier content is to generate, the more important durable proof becomes.

By Jason Colapietro (Johnny Suede) · Founder & CEO of Suede Labs AI

Why it matters in the AI era

AI upgraded creation speed. It did not upgrade ownership certainty. That gap is where attribution breaks, rights blur, consent becomes harder to verify, and authentic work gets harder to distinguish from imitation.

How Suede approaches proof

Suede treats proof as infrastructure. Creation becomes verifiable, ownable, and programmable instead of remaining a loose claim attached to content after distribution.
